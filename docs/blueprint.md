# **App Name**: OrgCentral Simplified

## Core Features:

- Landing Page: Landing page with key features and pricing information.
- Authentication: User registration and login functionality.
- Dashboard: Dashboard displaying organizational metrics and project overview.
- Project Management: Project listing with create, read, update, and delete (CRUD) operations.
- Project Details: Detailed project view with task management (CRUD operations).
- User Management: User management with user listing and basic CRUD operations.
- AI Role Suggestion: AI-powered role suggestion during user creation/editing. The LLM will act as a tool, providing suggestions based on job description analysis.
- Project Planning and Scheduling: Project planning and scheduling with CRUD operations, freeze operation, micro level schedules and its analysis
- Document Management: Pre construction documents  such as bidding proposals,contracts ,legal agreements,permits,site surverys , environmental repots with version for each of one
- Design & Drawing: Design & drawing blue prints ,2ddrawings viewer and editor ,specification for task or line item,3d models viewer and editor and close out like warranties,as-build drawings and final inscpection documents
- Inventory Management for Materials: Implement an integrated inventory management system that tracks the usage, availability, and reorder levels of construction materials. The system should provide real-time updates and forecasts to optimize procurement costs and ensure timely availability of materials.
- Skilled Workforce Allocation: Develop a dynamic database of the workforce that includes details about their skills, experience, and past project performance. Use this database to efficiently assign the right personnel to appropriate tasks, balancing the need for skilled labor with the availability of resources.
- Equipment Tracking and Management: Whether owned or rented, maintaining a comprehensive log of equipment is crucial. This system should record details about the equipment's efficiency, maintenance schedule, cost, and availability. For rented equipment, track the rental agreements, costs, and timelines to optimize usage and avoid penalties.
- Cost Management: Establish a cost management module that records and analyzes the procurement and operational costs of materials, labor, and equipment. This module can help in identifying cost overruns, potential savings, and financial forecasting.
- Supplier and Contractor Coordination: Enhance collaboration tools to seamlessly connect suppliers and contractors. This includes real-time communication, document sharing, and contract management functionalities to ensure that all parties are aligned with the project's goals and timelines.
- Compliance and Quality Check: Integrate a system for regular checks and compliance to standards for all procured items and services. This ensures that materials and construction practices meet the required standards and regulations, hence avoiding potential project delays and legal issues.
- Performance Analytics: Utilize data analytics to assess the performance and efficiency of resources. This analysis can help in making informed decisions about future projects and resource allocation strategies.
- Training and Development: Regular training sessions for the workforce can be incorporated to enhance skills and performance. Keeping the workforce updated with the latest construction techniques and safety protocols ensures higher productivity and adherence to quality.
- Sustainability Practices: Implement practices that emphasize sustainability in the procurement and utilization of resources. This includes opting for eco-friendly materials and techniques, which can also cater to the growing demand for green buildings.
- Technology Integration: Leverage advanced technologies such as AI, IoT, and blockchain to optimize resource management. These technologies can streamline processes, enhance transparency, and improve the accuracy of tracking and managing resources.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vivid blue (#4285F4) to evoke trust and professionalism, while still feeling modern.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F5F5), nearly white, for a clean and spacious feel.
- Accent color: Complementary orange (#FFA000) to highlight key actions and elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' sans-serif for a clean and modern user interface.
- Simple, geometric icons from a set like Material Design Icons for a consistent and modern look.
- Clean and structured layout with clear sections and intuitive navigation. Use of ShadCN UI components to ensure consistency and responsiveness.
- Subtle transitions and animations to provide feedback and enhance user experience, but avoiding distracting or unnecessary effects.
